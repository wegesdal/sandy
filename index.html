Welcome to sandy.network

Mission Statement

This site was created as a kid and mobile friendly web-development playground for learners of all ages. Because it can be reasonably assumed that this site will have visitors under the age of 13, it maintains strict privacy and community guidelines to protect its users.

Privacy Policy

This site does not collect or distribute personal information as defined by “COPPA”. This is defined as:
  • Full name
  • Physical or email address
  • User name where it function as online contact information
  • Telephone number
  • Social security number
  • Persistent identifier such as cookie, IP address, processor or device serial number
  • Photo, video, or audio file containing a child’s image or voice
  • Geolocation information sufficient to identify a street name and city or town
This site is actively monitored to ensure that users are abiding by this policy. Projects or assets containing personal information as defined above will be removed.

Community Guidelines

In addition to the privacy policy outlined above, and bearing in mind that this site is intended as a resource for learners of all ages, users are expected to post only content that:
  • is appropriate for users of all ages and backgrounds.
  • does not encourage or explain how to commit illegal or illicit acts.
  • does not advertise, link to social media, or otherwise create avenues for compromising user safety.
Site administrators reserve the right to remove content that does not meet this criteria.
